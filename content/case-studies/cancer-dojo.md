# Cancer Dojo

Cancer Dojo is an app that empowers cancer sufferers to play an active role in their cancer treatments, with the intention of making them more resilient to the negative effects of the disease.

# TL;DR

Cancer Dojo is unique in the sense that it is entirely CMS driven.

# Introduction

Great interactive app that puts the owner in control with page structuring and content types, also allows for easing adding of new content types for future development.

# Challenges

* CMS
* Bypassing payments with a custom vouchering system
* Adding different CMS content types in future.
* deeplinking with dynamic pages
